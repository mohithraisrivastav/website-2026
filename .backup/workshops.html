The One-Day Immersion

A single day of guided noticing. Participants are walked through a chosen site — a fort wall, a laterite quarry, a monsoon courtyard — and taught to slow down, listen, and record. The goal is not mastery but the first shift: from looking to seeing.

Practice
Guided site walk, sensory drills, journaling

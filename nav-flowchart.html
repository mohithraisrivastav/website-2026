Three primary entries — each a conceptual room. About and Contact become secondary (footer + hamburger menu). Dropdown or mega-menu reveals sub-pages on hover.

Hover / tap behaviour

On desktop, hovering See opens a minimal dropdown: Work — Film. No mega-menu, just two lines.

Active state

The active primary door stays highlighted in orange. Sub-page tabs (Prints / Books / Tools…) appear inside the page, not in the top nav.

Mobile

Hamburger opens a full-screen overlay with the three rooms as large italic headings. Sub-pages nested under each, disclosed on tap.

Label alternatives

See / Learn / Collect is one option. Others: Work / Words / Shop — or keep literal: Work + Film / Workshops + Journal / Shop.

About + Contact

Moved to footer (always reachable) and the hamburger overlay. Removing them from primary nav cuts the cognitive load without hiding them.

URL structure unchanged

The grouping is purely navigational — no URL changes. work.html, film.html, shop.html all stay as-is. Zero redirect work.
